Our Story

A Love Story
With Eggs

Kaka's Egg Corner started with a simple belief - that eggs, when made with care and passion, can be the most satisfying food in the world. Located in the vibrant Mansarover neighbourhood of Jaipur, we've been cracking eggs and breaking expectations.

From a humble corner stall to Jaipur's top-rated egg destination with over 5,000 glowing reviews - our secret remains unchanged: fresh ingredients, bold spices, and genuine love for what we do.
